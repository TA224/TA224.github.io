🚀 Site Block: Block distractions and Boost productivity

Site Block is a Chrome extension that helps you stay focused by blocking distracting websites, scheduling focus sessions, and keeping your calendar in sync.

✨ Key Features

  • Block Distractions: Block websites manually or automatically during scheduled focus modes.
  • Google Calendar Sync: Seamlessly sync your focus modes with your Google Calendar.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Create daily or weekly recurring focus blocks.
  • Strict Mode: Lock your settings with a passcode to prevent bypassing focus sessions.
  • Block/Whitelist: Choose to block specific sites or allow only certain sites during focus mode.

📱 Get the Extension

Install Site Block from the Chrome Web Store
